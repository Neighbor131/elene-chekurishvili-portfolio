Alrighty! as I’ve mentioned in the first part of this project, my main goal was to design a landing page which would serve as a guide for new travelers as well as introducing the company and their ideals.

I decided to divide landing page into pieces, instead of pages, main interaction is through scrolling.

Intro: Why you should choose this travel agency.

Trips: Most popular trips of the particular season.

Explore packages:To suit clients’ different needs.

Testimonials: Reviews from previous clients.

‘Get to know’ page: Useful recourses and “everything you should know before you visit” type of brief info for first-time visitors.

News: News, expos and travel shows, where you can have a private or business talk with the representatives.

Newsletter: Main purpose of having a newsletter is to promote new products, seasonal sales or packages, maintain strong and healthy relationship with your users and prospects, but it shouldn’t be forced, so I decided to place it right above the footer, so the users will decide whether signing up is worth it or not.

Enquiry:On top of the page with the form and in the end of the page.

Footer: About, tours & services, contact.
