Side-by-side comparison of Stable Diffusion 1.5 CFG-1.0 (test update of 31/08/2022) and Dall-E 2

Read on Medium

Stable Diffusion new update offers a new, pretty nice feature “aspect” ratio feature. Instead of trying to go for a specific size, you can directly use “aspect" to get desired ratio. Slow-mode was fixed as well. Regarding the channel surfing, you won’t be able to do that anymore, or else you’ll bet put in the penalty box if you attempt to do concurrent image generation request — says the official announcement.

There are up to 48 channels dedicated to the testing, you can generate by simple command of /dream! in any of these channels

One piece of good news -- no more slow-mode! The bad news is that you can no longer simply surf the channels, as the bot will put you in the penalty box if you attempt to do concurrent image generation requests. Repeatedly clicking will cause your penalty box time to increase exponentially as an anti-spam feature.

This comparison doesn’t prove anything, it just shows how these two generate the same prompt, the following generations for Stable are all in CFG-1.0 and for Dalle2 — “firstborn". I tried to use as many different art movements, styles, perspectives, mediums, eras, as possible.
