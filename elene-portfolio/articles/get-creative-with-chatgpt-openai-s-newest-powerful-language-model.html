A couple of days ago OpenAI introduce ChatGPT, a state-of-the-art language processing AI. ChatGPT is a highly advanced chatbot that uses natural language processing to understand and respond to user input in a conversation-like manner. With its ability to understand and respond to a wide range of topics, ChatGPT offers a unique and engaging experience for users.

In the following article, I will have some common scenarios and answers that caught my attention, for example, its understanding or role-play, irony, creative thinking, suggestions, advice and etc… P.s. since I’m not a developer, I won’t be discussing anything code-related here.

Pricing

Currently, it’s free for everyone. As the official website states:

it’s a Free Research Preview: ChatGPT is optimized for dialogue. Our goal is to make AI systems more natural to interact with, and your feedback will help us improve our systems and make them safer.

Features

One of the key features of ChatGPT is its ability to generate human-like text. This means that when you chat with ChatGPT, you will be able to have a realistic conversation with it. ChatGPT is trained on a massive dataset of human conversation, which allows it to understand and respond to a wide range of topics. This makes ChatGPT a valuable tool for anyone looking to have a realistic conversation with a chatbot.

chatgpt

An important aspect of ChatGPT is its ability to learn from previous conversations. This means that the more you chat with ChatGPT, the better it will become at understanding and responding to your input.This makes for a truly personalized and engaging experience for users. Another unique feature of ChatGPT is its ability to generate text in a variety of styles and tones. Whether you want to generate formal, academic language or something more colloquial and conversational, ChatGPT has the ability to adapt to your needs. This makes it a versatile tool that can be used in a wide range of applications.

Limitations

One of the main limitations is that it is a machine learning model, so it can only provide responses based on the data it has been trained on. This means that it may not be able to provide accurate or complete answers to questions that fall outside of its training data.

Similar to Dalle-2 generated variants, the bot provides rephrased answers when attempting to generate the same prompt couple of times.

ChatGPT is sensitive to tweaks to the input phrasing or attempting the same prompt multiple times. For example, given one phrasing of a question, the model can claim to not know the answer, but given a slight rephrase, can answer correctly.

In conclusion, ChatGPT is a powerful and versatile language model that offers a range of useful features for developers and users. Its ability to generate human-like responses in real-time makes it a valuable tool for a variety of applications, from chatbots to language translation. Additionally, its open-source nature and large-scale training allow for continued improvement and customization. Overall, ChatGPT is a promising development in the field of natural language processing and is worth exploring for those interested in advancing their language-based projects.
